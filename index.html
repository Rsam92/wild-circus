TIGERS SHOW

Be impressed

Wild Circus is proud to present for our 2017 summer performance season Ryan Holder and his magnificent family of captive bread Bengal-Siberian tigers.
Take a walk on the wild side for some up close and personal time with these majestic animals. We are committed to the preservation of the tiger species through public education as well as entertainment for young and old alike.
The tigers will be on display all day long allowing our guests an opportunity to witness their majesty, their behaviors and unique personalities.
